Mission:

Animals are our passion, and we believe that all who come through our shelter deserve to find their forever home. Through our sponsor program, people like you can help lessen the costs of adoption and contribute to the shelter by sponsoring one of our lovely residents. This not only reduces their adoption fee (or waives it entirely), but it increases the chance of our friend being adopted to their forever home. This way, even those who aren't ready to take on a pet can still help our cause!
